# Requirements Engineering Skill

## Purpose
Provide automotive requirements management and traceability expertise for systematic requirements development and change management.

## Capabilities
- DOORS/DOORS Next configuration
- Requirements decomposition methodology
- Traceability matrix generation
- Change impact analysis
- Requirements quality checking (INCOSE guidelines)
- V&V matrix generation
- Compliance matrix for regulations
- Requirements interchange (ReqIF)

## Usage Guidelines
- Establish requirements hierarchy from customer to component level
- Maintain complete traceability to design and tests
- Analyze impact of requirement changes
- Validate requirements quality against INCOSE guidelines
- Generate compliance matrices for regulations
- Document rationale for derived requirements

## Dependencies
- IBM DOORS
- PTC Integrity
- Jama Connect
- Polarion

- VSE-002: Requirements Engineering and Management
