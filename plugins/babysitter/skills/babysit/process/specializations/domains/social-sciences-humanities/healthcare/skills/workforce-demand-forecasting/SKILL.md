# Workforce Demand Forecasting

Project healthcare workforce requirements based on patient volume trends, acuity levels, productivity standards, and skill mix optimization.

## Overview

This skill enables forecasting of healthcare workforce needs. It encompasses demand analysis, staffing model development, and resource planning to ensure appropriate staffing levels aligned with patient care requirements.

## Capabilities

### Demand Analysis
- Volume forecasting
- Acuity trending
- Seasonality patterns
- Growth projections
- Service line analysis

### Staffing Models
- Hours per patient day
- Productivity standards
- Skill mix ratios
- Coverage requirements
- Contingency planning

### Resource Planning
- FTE calculations
- Budget projections
- Recruitment planning
- Skill gap analysis
- Succession planning

### Optimization
- Flex staffing strategies
- Float pool management
- Agency utilization
- Overtime management
- Cross-training opportunities

## Usage Guidelines

### Forecasting Process
1. Analyze historical data
2. Identify trends and patterns
3. Project future volumes
4. Adjust for known changes
5. Calculate staffing needs
6. Develop staffing plan
7. Monitor and adjust

### Key Inputs
- Patient volumes by unit
- Acuity/case mix data
- Productivity benchmarks
- Quality requirements
- Budget constraints

### Output Deliverables
- Staffing projections
- FTE requirements
- Budget forecasts
- Scenario analyses
- Implementation plans
