# Film & TV Production - Processes Backlog

## Future Process Ideas

This document tracks potential processes to be developed for the film-tv-production specialization.

---

## Short-Form Processes

### single-scene-writing
**Purpose:** Quick single scene creation
**Inputs:** Scene brief, characters, context
**Outputs:** Formatted scene in Fountain
**Use Case:** Rapid scene drafts, spec samples

### commercial-production
**Purpose:** Complete 15-60 second commercial
**Inputs:** Product, message, target audience
**Outputs:** Script, storyboard, video prompts
**Use Case:** Ad creation for AI video

### music-video-production
**Purpose:** Music video from song
**Inputs:** Song/track, visual concept
**Outputs:** Storyboard, shot list, video prompts
**Use Case:** Visual companion to music

## TV-Specific Processes

### tv-series-bible
**Purpose:** Complete series bible for ongoing show
**Inputs:** Series concept, pilot
**Outputs:** World bible, character arcs, episode templates
**Use Case:** Series pitch preparation

### episode-production
**Purpose:** Single episode within existing series
**Inputs:** Series bible, episode premise
**Outputs:** Episode script, production materials
**Use Case:** Episode-by-episode production

### season-arc-planning
**Purpose:** Plan complete season arc
**Inputs:** Series bible, season themes
**Outputs:** Episode breakdowns, character arcs, cliffhangers
**Use Case:** Season planning for serialized content

## Enhancement Processes

### script-polish
**Purpose:** Dialogue and action polish pass
**Inputs:** Existing screenplay
**Outputs:** Polished screenplay with notes
**Use Case:** Script improvement

### coverage-expansion
**Purpose:** Expand shot coverage
**Inputs:** Existing screenplay, visual style
**Outputs:** Detailed shot lists, additional angles
**Use Case:** Pre-production planning

### character-deepening
**Purpose:** Deepen existing characters
**Inputs:** Character profiles, story context
**Outputs:** Enhanced profiles, backstory, voice
**Use Case:** Character development pass
