# Entrepreneurship and Startup Processes - References

A curated collection of authoritative resources for entrepreneurs, startup founders, and those building new ventures.

---

## Books

### Startup Methodology and Lean Startup

1. **The Lean Startup: How Today's Entrepreneurs Use Continuous Innovation to Create Radically Successful Businesses**
   - Author: Eric Ries
   - Publisher: Currency
   - URL: https://theleanstartup.com/book
   - Description: Foundational methodology for building startups through validated learning, rapid experimentation, and iterative product development.

2. **The Four Steps to the Epiphany: Successful Strategies for Products That Win**
   - Author: Steve Blank
   - Publisher: K&S Ranch
   - URL: https://www.steveblank.com/books/
   - Description: The original Customer Development methodology manual, introducing the systematic approach to searching for a repeatable business model.

3. **The Startup Owner's Manual: The Step-by-Step Guide for Building a Great Company**
   - Authors: Steve Blank and Bob Dorf
   - Publisher: K&S Ranch
   - URL: https://www.steveblank.com/books/
   - Description: Comprehensive handbook for Customer Development with detailed checklists and processes for building startups.

4. **Running Lean: Iterate from Plan A to a Plan That Works**
   - Author: Ash Maurya
   - Publisher: O'Reilly Media
   - URL: https://leanstack.com/running-lean-book
   - Description: Practical guide to applying Lean Startup principles with the Lean Canvas and systematic experimentation.

5. **Lean Analytics: Use Data to Build a Better Startup Faster**
   - Authors: Alistair Croll and Benjamin Yoskovitz
   - Publisher: O'Reilly Media
   - URL: https://leananalyticsbook.com/
   - Description: Data-driven approach to startup metrics, focusing on the One Metric That Matters for each stage.

### Business Model and Value Proposition

6. **Business Model Generation: A Handbook for Visionaries, Game Changers, and Challengers**
   - Authors: Alexander Osterwalder and Yves Pigneur
   - Publisher: Wiley
   - URL: https://www.strategyzer.com/books/business-model-generation
   - Description: The definitive guide to the Business Model Canvas with practical tools and examples.

7. **Value Proposition Design: How to Create Products and Services Customers Want**
   - Authors: Alexander Osterwalder, Yves Pigneur, Gregory Bernarda, and Alan Smith
   - Publisher: Wiley
   - URL: https://www.strategyzer.com/books/value-proposition-design
   - Description: Deep dive into value proposition development using the Value Proposition Canvas.

8. **Testing Business Ideas: A Field Guide for Rapid Experimentation**
   - Authors: David J. Bland and Alexander Osterwalder
   - Publisher: Wiley
   - URL: https://www.strategyzer.com/books/testing-business-ideas
   - Description: Practical guide to designing and running experiments to test business ideas quickly.

9. **The Invincible Company: How to Constantly Reinvent Your Organization**
   - Authors: Alexander Osterwalder, Yves Pigneur, Fred Etiemble, and Alan Smith
   - Publisher: Wiley
   - URL: https://www.strategyzer.com/books/the-invincible-company
   - Description: Framework for building companies that continuously explore new business models while exploiting existing ones.
