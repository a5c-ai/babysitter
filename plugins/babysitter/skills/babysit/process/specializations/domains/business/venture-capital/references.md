# Venture Capital and Investment Due Diligence References

This document provides a curated collection of authoritative references for the Venture Capital and Investment Due Diligence specialization, including books, standards, professional associations, thought leaders, and educational resources.

## Books

### Venture Capital Fundamentals

1. **Venture Deals: Be Smarter Than Your Lawyer and Venture Capitalist** by Brad Feld and Jason Mendelson
   - The definitive guide to venture capital term sheets and deal structures
   - https://www.venturedeals.com/

2. **The Business of Venture Capital** by Mahendra Ramsinghani
   - Comprehensive guide to VC fund formation, operations, and investing
   - https://www.wiley.com/en-us/The+Business+of+Venture+Capital%2C+3rd+Edition-p-9781119639688

3. **Mastering the VC Game** by Jeffrey Bussgang
   - Insider perspective on how VCs think and how founders can succeed
   - https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/301428/mastering-the-vc-game-by-jeffrey-bussgang/

4. **Secrets of Sand Hill Road** by Scott Kupor
   - A16Z partner shares insights on raising venture capital
   - https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/572024/secrets-of-sand-hill-road-by-scott-kupor/

5. **Venture Capital and the Finance of Innovation** by Andrew Metrick and Ayako Yasuda
   - Academic treatment of VC economics and deal structures
   - https://www.wiley.com/en-us/Venture+Capital+and+the+Finance+of+Innovation%2C+3rd+Edition-p-9781119490111

### Private Equity and Buyouts

6. **Private Equity at Work** by Eileen Appelbaum and Rosemary Batt

7. **The Masters of Private Equity and Venture Capital** by Robert Finkel

8. **Investment Banking: Valuation, LBOs, M&A, and IPOs** by Joshua Rosenbaum and Joshua Pearl
   - Comprehensive guide to valuation and transaction analysis
   - https://www.wiley.com/en-us/Investment+Banking%3A+Valuation%2C+LBOs%2C+M%26A%2C+and+IPOs%2C+3rd+Edition-p-9781119706182

9. **Private Equity: History, Governance, and Operations** by Harry Cendrowski et al.
   - Deep dive into PE fund structure and operations
   - https://www.wiley.com/en-us/Private+Equity%3A+History%2C+Governance%2C+and+Operations%2C+2nd+Edition-p-9781118138502

10. **King of Capital: The Remarkable Rise, Fall, and Rise Again of Steve Schwarzman and Blackstone** by David Carey and John E. Morris
    - History of one of the world's largest PE firms
    - https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/303469/king-of-capital-by-david-carey-and-john-e-morris/
